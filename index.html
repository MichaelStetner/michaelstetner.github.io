Who Am I?



I'm completing my PhD in neuroscience from MIT where I'm studying how birds control their body movements. I designed a miniature microdrive with a gyroscope and analog amplifiers to record head orientation and neural activity simultaneously. I love tinkering and data analysis, and I'm looking for a job where I can continue to do both.
